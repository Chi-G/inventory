# Elevate Interiors Inventory System - User Guide

This guide provides an overview of the key features and workflows within the Elevate Interiors Inventory Management system.

---

## 1. Dashboard Overview
The Dashboard is your primary command center. It provides real-time insights into your business health:
- **Total Catalog**: Total number of unique products in your system.
- **Stock Value**: The current monetary value of your inventory (Cost vs. Retail).
- **Low Stock Alerts**: Displays products currently below their threshold, requiring immediate attention.
- **Recent Activity**: A live feed of all stock movements (In/Out/Adjustments).

## 2. Product Management
The Catalog is where you manage the master record of your physical items.
- **Adding Products**: Define SKU, Name, Category, Pricing, and Alert Thresholds.
- **Visuals**: Upload product images to help staff identify items quickly.
- **Exporting**: Use the "Export CSV" button to download a spreadsheet of your entire inventory for reporting or backup.

## 3. The Barcode System
The system is built to bridge the gap between software and your physical shelves.
- **Automatic Generation**: Every product is assigned a **Code 128** barcode. If not manually set, it defaults to the product's SKU.
- **Printing Labels**: 
    1. Go to the Product Catalog.
    2. Click the `...` menu on any product.
    3. Select **"Print Label"**.
    4. A dedicated product sheet will open and trigger the print dialog. 
- **Scanning**: Use the **Scanner Center** to instantly look up a product by scanning its barcode. You can also use a standard USB scanner on any input field.

## 4. Stock Movements & Inventory Control
Maintaining accurate stock levels is critical.
- **Stock-In**: Records items arriving from suppliers. You can link these movements to specific suppliers for better tracking.
- **Stock-Out**: Records items leaving the inventory (sales or transfers).
- **Adjustments**: Used for corrections following as physical audit or to account for damaged goods.
- **Inventory Logs**: View a complete history of every single unit that has entered or left the building.

## 5. Administration & Security
(Admin Only)
- **User Management**: Add or remove staff members and assign them slugs for personalized access.
- **Role-Based Permissions**: Control exactly who can view, edit, delete, or adjust stock. 
- **Clean Interface**: The application uses a standardized premium light-themed design to ensure high readability in warehouse or office environments.
